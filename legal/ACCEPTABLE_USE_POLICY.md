1. **Infringes rights** — content you do not own or are not licensed to edit,

2. **Lacks required consent** — footage of identifiable people processed for

3. **Is illegal or harmful** — child sexual abuse material (CSAM), content that
   sexualizes minors, non-consensual intimate imagery, or content that
   facilitates a crime.
4. **Is deceptive** — deepfakes, manipulated media, or synthetic content created
   to impersonate a real person or mislead about real events, especially in
   electoral, financial, or public-safety contexts.
5. **Promotes harm** — incites violence, terrorism, self-harm, or unlawful
   discrimination or harassment against a protected group.
6. **Violates privacy** — exposes others' personal or sensitive information
   without authorization.

9. **Reverse engineer** or attempt to extract underlying models or source where
   prohibited by the Terms or applicable law.
10. Use the Service to **build a competing dataset or model** from our outputs in

11. Resell or provide the Service to third parties except as expressly permitted
