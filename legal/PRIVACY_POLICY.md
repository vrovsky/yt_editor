## 3. Third-party AI processing (important)

If AI generation is enabled, the **text transcript** of your video and edit
metadata are sent to our large-language-model provider (see Subprocessors) to
refine the edit. Your raw video is **not** sent to the LLM provider. We use the
provider under terms that prohibit using your data to train their models.

You can avoid all third-party LLM processing by using the deterministic editing
mode, which runs entirely on our infrastructure.
